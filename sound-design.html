üé® Sound Design

This section showcases a collection of short films and audio tracks sound-designed by me. The audio clips originate from various sources: 1) Recordings using a Zoom H4N Pro Recorder; 2) My own sound synthesis using Spectrasonics Omnisphere software synthesizer; 3) Samples selected from free sound libraries.

The entirety of this sound design work was accomplished utilizing Logic Pro and Pro Tools. Additionally, the background music for "Last Shot" and "RingTone" was skillfully arranged using the free loop package available in Logic Pro.
