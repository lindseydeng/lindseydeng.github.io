HARP 3.0

HARP is a sample editor for hosted, asynchronous, remote processing of audio with machine learning. HARP operates as a standalone application or a plugin-like editor within your DAW, and routes audio, MIDI, and metadata through Gradio endpoints for processing. Gradio apps can be hosted locally or remotely (e.g., HuggingFace Spaces), allowing users of DAWs to access powerful cloud-based models seamlessly from within their DAW.

This is a collaborative project with Interative Audio Lab at Northwestern University and Audio Information Research Group at University of Rochester. My contributions include work of generalized API support, expanding models, and the improvement other features.
